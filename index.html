Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm a beginner?

The workout is entirely tailored to you and your goals. If you are new to fitness, we've got your back with a personalized program designed specifically for beginners. You'll start with appropriate weights and exercises for your fitness level.

How long until I see results?

Most clients start seeing visible changes within 4-6 weeks of consistent training. However, you'll feel stronger and more energetic within the first two weeks. Remember, consistency is key to achieving lasting results.

Do I need special equipment?

Our home workout program requires minimal equipment (resistance bands, a few dumbbells). For gym programs, any standard gym equipment will be sufficient. We provide alternatives for each exercise based on what you have available.

How is this different from other programs?

Our program combines Vlada's functional training expertise with Mehdi's strength-focused approach, creating a unique methodology that emphasizes proper form, progressive overload, and sustainable results. We focus on long-term success, not quick fixes.

Is nutrition guidance included?

Yes, all our programs include basic nutrition guidelines. Our premium packages include personalized meal plans tailored to your specific goals, dietary preferences, and lifestyle needs.

How do I access the workout content?

After purchase, you'll receive immediate access to our private member portal where all your workout videos, PDFs, and resources are stored. You can access this content on any device, anytime, anywhere.
